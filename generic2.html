Volleyball Passing Drils

Use the proper stance.You want to stand light with your feet little further slightly more than shoulder-width apart, forcing yur butt to stick out a little and you should be standing on yur toes and ready to move in any direction in a split second Using yur proper stance extend yur arms straight in front of you. Now bend yur elbows half way.This make it possible to pass with either your forearms or yur hands in a split second decision Learn to pass with yur stand. Now that you have yur ready position down, work on the form for passing with your forearms, or stand. Take one hand and place it flat on the other. Next, take your thumbs and have them meet in the middle. Cup your fingers and point your thumbs away from you. Try to get your thumbs as far from your body as possible, this will straighten your arms and force your forearms up to create your stand. You always want your stand to be straight.

You always want the ball to go back into the court when you’re passing. Therefore, whichever leg is closest the outside of the court will be the one you lead with. This will face your stand toward the court.Keep your eye on the ball. Watch the ball come over the net and onto your stand. Never look away from the ball.Swinging your arms creates inconsistency in where your pass will go. You need very strong legs to stay low and pass correctly.Follow through to target as you straighten your legs to move the ball, be aware of the way you’re facing. If you’re facing the target, you’ve done it correctly.
